We don't just treat symptoms. We take time to understand your lifestyle, history, and goals, then craft a customized care plan designed to achieve lasting results — not just temporary relief.

Meet Dr. Sarah Mitchell

Dr

Dr. Sarah Mitchell

Doctor of Chiropractic, D.C.

Palmer College Graduate ACA Member Sports Certified Webster Technique

"I chose chiropractic because I believe the body has an incredible capacity to heal itself — it just sometimes needs a little guidance."

Dr. Mitchell completed her Doctor of Chiropractic degree at Palmer College of Chiropractic, one of the nation's most respected chiropractic institutions. She has pursued advanced training in sports injury rehabilitation, prenatal care, and pediatric chiropractic.

With over 15 years of clinical experience, she has helped thousands of patients — from weekend warriors to professional athletes to seniors — rediscover what it feels like to move without pain.
