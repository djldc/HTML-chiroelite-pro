Every Service Designed Around Your Recovery

We offer a complete spectrum of chiropractic and wellness services — all under one roof. Whether you're recovering from an injury or investing in long-term health, we have a treatment plan for you.

Our Core Service

Spinal Adjustments

Chiropractic adjustments — also called spinal manipulation — are the cornerstone of what we do. Using precise, controlled force applied to specific joints, we restore proper spinal alignment, relieve nerve pressure, and unlock your body's natural healing potential.

We employ multiple adjustment techniques tailored to each patient, including Diversified, Thompson Drop, Activator, and Gonstead methods.

Back Pain Neck Pain Headaches Sciatica Herniated Discs

Active Patients

Sports Injury Treatment

Whether you're a competitive athlete or a recreational gym-goer, sports injuries can sideline your training and your life. We specialize in diagnosing and treating sports-related musculoskeletal injuries with a focus on accelerated recovery and performance optimization.

Our sports injury protocol combines chiropractic adjustments, soft-tissue therapy, and corrective exercises — getting you back to peak performance faster.

Sprains & Strains Runner's Knee Shoulder Injuries IT Band Syndrome Tennis Elbow

Complementary Therapy

Massage Therapy

Massage therapy and chiropractic care are a powerful combination. Our licensed massage therapists specialize in techniques that prepare muscles and soft tissues for adjustment, reduce recovery time, and extend the benefits of your chiropractic care.

Available as standalone sessions or as part of a combined chiropractic + massage care plan.

Deep Tissue Trigger Point Swedish Massage Myofascial Release Prenatal Massage

Injury Documentation

Auto Accident Recovery

Auto accidents — even minor ones — can cause significant spinal and soft-tissue injuries that may not surface for hours or days. Early chiropractic intervention is critical for both your recovery and your legal documentation.

We work directly with auto insurance companies and personal injury attorneys. Our detailed documentation supports your claim, and we accept personal injury liens in many cases.

Whiplash Neck Injury Back Injury Insurance Accepted PIP Claims

Long-Term Health

Wellness Programs

Pain relief is just the beginning. Our wellness programs are designed to maintain the gains you've made, prevent future injuries, and support your overall health — so you can live with energy, freedom, and confidence every single day.

Customized wellness plans include periodic adjustments, exercise prescriptions, nutritional guidance, and ergonomic coaching.

Preventive Care Posture Correction Ergonomics Nutrition Guidance Exercise Plans

Family Care

Pediatric Chiropractic

Children's spines are subject to significant stress from birth, through childhood development, sports, and heavy backpacks. Pediatric chiropractic care uses extremely gentle, low-force techniques that are completely safe and highly effective for children of all ages.

Many parents bring children in for colic, ear infections, posture issues, sports injuries, and general wellness support.
